About Us

We are located in one of the prime areas of Delhi - Saket and we offer two plush, pillar-less banquet halls for weddings, anniversaries, birthday parties, corporate events and other social events. The prime location makes it easily accessible bymetro and other modes of public transport.

We also have an experienced team of chefs on board who can whip up delicaciesfrom every cuisine to complement any occasion or taste. Our professional teamof decorators offer beautiful decor setups in every budget, which make the partyareas more stunning. If you have outstation guests coming in, we also havecomfortable rooms above the banquet which are perfect for your loved ones. Weoffer all the best amenities and all the required services, which make us one ofthe best wedding venues in town.

Party Areas

We have two banquet halls which can accommodate 300 people each in floating style. The ground floor banquet hall is also attached to a lawn, which makes it perfect for open air weddings, lavish receptions and cocktail parties. If you have smaller functions, the banquet hall on the first floor can be partitioned to form small party areas.

The separate entrances for these party areas make hosting multiple parties simultaneously at Lagoona Emerald an easy job. Our unmatched services are complemented by the warm and hospitable staff, who are extremely well-trained in hospitality.
